Standards followed: PK.MD.2 & PK.CC.1

Objective: Match 2 objects that are exactly the same.

Count to 2 Chant

Say it with me:

1, 2, I count 2

Interactive Matching Game

Student Debrief

Reflection Questions:

  • How did you choose the matching items?
  • Can you find anything around you that matches?
  • Complete the sentence: "These objects are exactly the same. They are both _______."
